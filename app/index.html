Accommodations

Rooms are available for guests at the Mauna Kea Beach Hotel and its sister property next door, the Hapuna Beach Prince Hotel. Both hotels are approximately 40 minutes from the airport and 20 minutes from the wedding venue, in the Kohala Coast region of the Big Island.

Mauna Kea Beach Hotel

  • Golf Vista $330/night
  • Deluxe Golf Vista $350/night
  • Beach Front $450/night
  • Ocean View $460/night
  • Deluxe Ocean View $660/night

Hapuna Beach Prince Hotel

  • Terrace/Partial Ocean View $230/night
  • Ocean View $260/night
  • Premium Ocean View $280/night
  • Ocean Front $330/night

All rooms/rates are subject to availability for stays between April 27 and May 6, 2014. Call 1-866-PRINCE-6 (1-866-774-6236) and ask for the Kotin/Crouse wedding guest rates when booking. Complimentary self-parking is extended to all wedding guests.

If you plan to stay for an extended vacation, you may want to consider renting a condo. We recommend searching the Kohala Coast area (from Waikoloa to Hawi) on AirBnB or VRBO to find the best deals.
