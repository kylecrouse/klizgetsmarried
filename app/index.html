Accommodations

We recommend looking at accommodations in the Kohala Coast area—from Waikaloa to Hawi. In Waikaloa, which is a bit farther from the wedding venue, there are a number of accommodation options, including the Waikoloa Beach Marriott Resort & Spa, the Hilton Waikaloa Village and the Outrigger Fairway Villas.

There are two hotel options closer to the wedding venue in Waimea: the Mauna Kea Beach Hotel and the Hapuna Beach Prince Hotel. The hotels are sister properties—located about two miles apart—and are approximately 40 minutes from the airport.

Discounted rates are available for our wedding guests at both the Mauna Kea Beach Hotel and the Hapuna Beach Prince Hotel for the room types listed below.

If you're planning an extended trip to Hawaii, you may want to consider renting a condo. We recommend searching the Kohala Coast area on AirBnB or VRBO to find the best deals.

Getting to the Hotels

If you are renting a car at the Kona airport, follow these directions to find the Mauna Kea and Hapuna Prince hotels:

  • As you leave the Airport, turn left onto Queen Kaahumanu Highway 19, heading toward Kawaihae.
  • Drive north about 27 miles, approximately 30 minutes.
  • After about 15 minutes you will come to a stoplight (near the Waikoloa Resort area), from this point you are half way there.
  • When you see the signs for the small town of Puako, keep going. You have about 2 miles left.
  • You'll pass a state highway sign for Hapuna Beach State Park. Continue over the overpass. You're getting close!
  • If you're staying at the Hapuna Beach Prince Hotel, the entrance will be shortly after the overpass on the right. Enter the driveway and the guard will direct you to the hotel.
  • If you're staying at the Mauna Kea Beach Hotel, keep driving for approximately one mile until you see a lava rock wall with "Mauna Kea" on the left side. As you turn into the driveway, you'll approach the gatehouse and hotel staff will direct you to the hotel.
